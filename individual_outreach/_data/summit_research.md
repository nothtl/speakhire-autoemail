## Run of Show

### 4:00–5:00 - The Summit Welcome & Exploration
Network with **industry partners**, explore **college pathways**, and engage with the Queens Museum's world-class exhibits. This is the career fair / exploration portion where companies and colleges have tables and students can make direct connections.

### 5:00–6:00 - The SpeakHire Heroes Leadership Panel
A dynamic panel featuring 3 distinguished Career Pathways Champions discussing:
- Habits that drive professional excellence
- Strategies for navigating career challenges  
- Advocacy skills
- Navigating professional futures in an AI-driven job market
- Overcoming economic shifts
- Leading with resilience

### 6:00–7:00 - Celebration & Community Connections
Dinner and reception. Music, dance, and meaningful networking. Celebrating the future of the workforce.
